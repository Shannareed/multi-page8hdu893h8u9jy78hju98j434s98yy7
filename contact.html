FAQ

  1. When Do You Ship?
    All items will be shipped within **2-3 business days** from the date of purchase. We strive to ensure timely and efficient delivery, providing you with the highest standard of service.

  3. Are your products covered by a warranty?
    Many of our products come with a manufacturer’s warranty. Specific warranty details can be found on the product page or by contacting our customer service team for more information.
  4. Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
    Orders can only be modified or canceled within a short window after placing them. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible for assistance.
  5. How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the “Sign Up” or “Register” button on our website, and follow the prompts to enter your details. Having an account allows you to track orders, save favorite items, and enjoy a faster checkout process.
  6. Do you offer international shipping?
    Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary based on your location. Please check our Shipping Information page for more details.
  7. How can I contact customer service?
    You can reach our customer service team via email at [email address], through our contact form on the website, or by calling [phone number] during business hours.
  8. What is your return and exchange policy?
    We offer a [number]-day return/exchange policy. Items must be returned in their original condition, with tags attached. For more details, please refer to our Return Policy page.
  9. How can I track my order?
    Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your shipment. You can also check the status of your order by logging into your account on our website.

Creator of the Brand

Creator of the Brand
Shanna Reed
Email me at Shaylashaniya@yahoo.com
I grew up in California, where I developed a passion for fashion at a young age. As a Black woman in a world where representation matters, I often found myself searching for accessories that aligned with my style and identity. My journey to entrepreneurship started with a simple idea: to create something that embodied the confidence and elegance I wanted to see. I envisioned a handbag line that would serve as both a fashion statement and a form of self-expression. This dream led me to create *Shhh Shoetique*, a brand dedicated to bold, elegant, and versatile handbags for women who embrace their unique style.

Starting this business was far from easy. I faced a range of obstacles, from securing funding as a minority woman in a male-dominated industry, to navigating the complexities of the fashion world without a formal background in business. There were moments when I questioned whether I had what it took to succeed. But every setback, from financial hardships to overcoming societal expectations, became a lesson in perseverance. I was determined not only to create beautiful, high-quality products but also to carve out a space for women like me in the industry. I surrounded myself with mentors, learned the ins and outs of design and manufacturing, and refused to give up on my vision.

Today, *Shhh Shoetique* has blossomed into more than just a handbag brand—it’s a symbol of empowerment. Every piece in our collection represents resilience, ambition, and the power of dreams. Through hard work, creativity, and a relentless belief in myself, I’ve established my brand in the competitive world of fashion, and I’m proud to be an example for other women, especially women of color, aspiring to break barriers in entrepreneurship. My journey is far from over, but I know that the struggles I’ve faced have only made me stronger and more determined to continue growing and expanding my business.
